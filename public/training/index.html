Training Courses

Introduction

This set of courses are designed to teach concepts of Data Science and how to use statistical and machine learning methods to solve real business problems with Python. Courses come with a book with theory, exercises, Jupyter notebooks with practical examples, instructions how to install software and how to use it. The courses are designed to be interactive and practical. The students will learn by doing, by solving real business problems.

Strategic Courses for Business Leaders

Data Science and AI for Business Leaders
A comprehensive, business-oriented introduction to Data Science, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI. Designed for executives and decision makers, this course focuses on strategy, ROI, governance, and effective communication with technical teams.

Technical Courses

Introduction to Python
This course provides a practical introduction to programming with Python, focusing on essential skills for data, automation, and scripting. Ideal for beginners, it emphasizes hands-on exercises and real-world examples to build confidence and fluency in Python programming.
Data Analysis and Reporting
Learn how to read, clean, analyze, and report on data using Python. This course covers the main libraries for data analysis and visualization, guiding you through the process of transforming raw data into clear, actionable reports and presentations.
Data Science
An introduction to machine learning and optimization with Python. Explore supervised and unsupervised learning, time series analysis, and optimization problems, with a focus on practical business applications and hands-on problem solving.
Professional Project Management
Master the essentials of managing data science projects with Python. This course covers project organization, version control, reproducibility, team collaboration, and deployment, helping you streamline and professionalize your workflow.
Deep Learning and Generative Models
Understand and apply deep learning and generative models to real business problems. Learn about neural networks, popular frameworks, and how to build custom models for regression, classification, and generative tasks.
Mastering Large Language Models with AI Agents
Explore the power of Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI agents. Learn to design, implement, and deploy intelligent agents that leverage LLMs for automation, information retrieval, and real-world business solutions.
