We are a senior AI product studio.
We help teams turn applied AI research into reliable, production-ready systems.

Why we exist

Bulding AI products is deceptively easy. Making them work in production, scale over time, and be trusted by users is not. We started Vanlog to bridge the gap between AI research, product design, and real-world constraints — data quality, evaluation, costs, and long-term maintenance.

How we work

  1. Understand the problem
    We start from the use case, not from the model. Constraints, users, risks, data availability.

  2. Measure before building
    Baselines, evaluations, and benchmarks come first. If we can’t measure it, we don’t ship it.

  3. Build for production
    Monitoring, logging, fallbacks, guardrails. No demos that break after launch.

  4. Iterate with evidence
    Improve quality, latency, and costs using real data. Decisions are driven by metrics, not hype.

  5. Transfer ownership
    Clear documentation, walkthroughs, and training. Teams are never left with a black box.

Principles

Evidence-first
Claims must be measurable.
Product over prototypes
Shipping matters more than novelty.
Long-term thinking
We design systems people can maintain.
