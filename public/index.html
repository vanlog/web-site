Senior AI Product Studio

Vanlog's mission is to unlock the value of your knowledge and workflows with custom AI agents.

We help businesses, public organizations, and research teams automate processes, accelerate decision-making, and empower people by connecting AI directly to your content and systems.

From discovery to deployment, Vanlog guides you through every step: opportunity mapping, agent and retrieval design, integration, evaluation, and handover. We deliver solutions you own and control.

Our team brings deep experience in AI, product design, and software engineering. We work hands-on with your experts to ensure every agent is robust, transparent, and aligned with your goals.

Evidence-first by default: answers with citations, predictable behavior, and clear limits. If you want to see what Vanlog can do, just get in touch.

Turn company knowledge into an actionable asset.

  • We ingest documents, PDFs, and shared folders (and, when needed, email threads and chat logs) into a unified, searchable knowledge base.
  • Teams get evidence-first answers with citations, plus summaries and checklists that follow your procedures.
  • Designed to stay up to date with your sources: no manual copy/paste knowledge management.

Automate workflows with AI agents.

  • We build AI agents that execute repeatable workflows by calling your APIs and tools—turning requests into actions.
  • Typical automations include ticket creation/updates, report generation, data entry, and notifications.
  • Evidence-first by default: agents log what they did and why, and can cite the sources they used.

Services

Agent Discovery (1 hour)

A structured 60-minute session to turn an AI agent idea into a concrete plan: use case, sources, integrations, risks, and next steps.

Custom AI Agents

We build custom AI agents end-to-end: a fast MVP to validate the use case, then engineering to production with integrations, guardrails, logging, documentation & knowledge transfer.

Workflow Automation Agents

AI agents that execute repeatable workflows by calling your tools and APIs—turning requests into actions (tickets, reports, data entry, notifications).
