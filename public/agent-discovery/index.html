Agent Discovery (1 hour)

In 60 minutes, we turn your idea into a concrete, actionable plan.

What you get: You receive a one-page summary with:

  • Use case tailored to your context
  • Data sources and required integrations
  • Identified risks and constraints
  • Next steps for your MVP

Who is it for?

  • Companies and teams exploring AI agents for the first time
  • Product owners and innovation leads with a specific challenge
  • Anyone who wants to move from idea to action, fast

What do you need?

  • A real business problem or opportunity
  • 60 minutes of your time (remote session)
  • Openness to discuss data, processes, and goals

What do you get?

  • A clear, actionable use case
  • List of data sources and integration points
  • Key risks and how to address them
  • Concrete next steps for your MVP
