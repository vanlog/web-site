Temperature Fluctuation Paper

This website is aimed at sharing all the materials related to the paper ”How does the temperature vary over time? Evidence on the stationary and fractal nature of temperature fluctuations”, by J.K. Dagsvik, M. Fortuna and S.H. Moen

Read the paper

Data

Temperature time series from all around the world, available as monthly figures + Moberg reconstructed temperature

Functions

R functions supporting the temperature paper: data exploration, stationarity and FGN testing, bootstrap simulations

Press

Divulgative version of the paper, press articles and ppresentations

Reproducible results

This section contains all the results and appendix of the Temperature paper, together with the code that generated them. See the results, read the code, and, if you want, download the materials and reproduce everything by yourself
